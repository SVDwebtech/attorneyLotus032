About

Who we are

Blunt Attorneys is a Johannesburg based imaginary legal practice which specialises in property, family and labour law.

Our attorneys are innovative and results driven, and strive to achieve the best results for our clients under circumstances which can be somewhat intimidating when facing it alone.

Our experienced attorneys will be there every step of the way with comprehensive advice, and will tackle your legal issues with the necessary skill and dedication to resolve your legal problems as switflty as possible.

Our success

Our team at Blunt Attorneys will take on any case, big or small. We have successfully litigated several complicated matters both in our jurisdiction in Johannesburg, as well as in Pretoria and several other major jurisdictions accross South Africa.

Your success is important to us and that is why your matter will be refered to the most experienced attorney in our firm in matters that relates to your legal battle.

Your matter will receive our highest attention to prioritise it in order to attain the best results and outcome.

Why us?

Experienced and professional

Our senior attorneys heading up the various divisions in our firm, have each more than 10 year’s experience in their related fields and most of them have served on various boards which indicates the faith in their abilities.

No obligation discussion

Feel free to schedule a 30 minute consultation at no cost to explore any issues regarding the matter you wish to clarify. We afford you the opportunity and information to decide whether you wish to commit to your case.

Pride and dedication

We pride ourselves on our dedication and commitment to you. We will relentlessly advocate on your behalf and will exhaust all your rememdies to ensure the best possible outcome.
